ABOUT US



Future Food is a critical look at the place where food and technology intersect. Both topics touch our lives everyday. Terms like fair trade, local, organic, cooperative, and plant-based are becoming a regular part of conversations about food. Similarly, apps that deliver food, connect people around food, and use advanced research to dramatically change the way that food is made are rolling out. Innovation is changing the way we eat, let’s take a closer look.



Giselle Sperber

user experience researcher and designer

Passionate about healthy communities and good food, she co-founded the Greene Hill Food Co-op, a member owned grocery story in Brooklyn. Giselle loves sharing food knowledge, and was a longtime Community Food Educator for the non-profit Just Food. She spent 2014 as a Code for America fellow working with Chattanooga, Tennessee, to improve local government.

Drew Wilson

developer, organizer, and researcher

Drew founded New England VegFest in 2009, a vegetarian food festival. He was previously the CTO of Compassion Over Killing — an organization dedicated to exposing cruelty to farm animals. In 2014 he put his passion for using his technical skills to improve communities to use as a Code for America fellow in Denver.
